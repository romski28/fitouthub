## 1. Platform Fee Disclosure (New Section)

### 1.1 fee Application
Fitout Hub ("Platform") applies a variable platform fee to all professional quotations before presentation to clients. The platform fee is added to the professional's submitted base quote amount to determine the price presented to the client.

**Professional's Perspective:**
- Professional submits base quote (the amount they propose for the work)
- Platform calculates and adds its fee; professional is notified of the gross amount
- Client receives only the gross price (base + platform fee)

**Client's Perspective:**
- Client receives a single quoted price (gross, including platform fee)
- Client does not see the fee breakdown unless explicitly shown by platform
- Client's contract and payment obligations are based on the gross quoted price


### 1.2 Fee Calculation Methodology

Platform fees are calculated using the following tiered structure based on quote amount:

| Quote Amount Range | Base Fee Percent |
|--------------------|------------------|
| HK$0 – HK$1,000    | 12%             |
| HK$1,001 – HK$10,000 | 10%             |
| HK$10,001 – HK$100,000 | 8%             |
| HK$100,001+        | 7%              |

#### Fee Adjustments
The base fee may be adjusted based on contractor performance and client loyalty:

**Professional Performance Adjustment:**
- 0–10 awarded projects: 0% adjustment
- 11–30 awarded projects: -1% fee reduction
- 31+ awarded projects: -2% fee reduction

**Client Loyalty Adjustment:**
- 0–5 historical projects: 0% adjustment
- 6–10 historical projects: -1% fee reduction
- 11+ historical projects: -2% fee reduction

**Example:**
- Professional submits base quote: HK$50,000
- Quote band base fee: 8% (falls in HK$10,001–100,000 range)
- Professional has 15 awarded projects: -1% adjustment
- Client has 8 historical projects: -1% adjustment
- Effective fee: 8% - 1% - 1% = 6%
- Fee amount: HK$50,000 × 6% = HK$3,000
- Client quoted price: HK$50,000 + HK$3,000 = HK$53,000
- Rounding (floored to nearest HK$10): HK$53,000 → HK$53,000


### 1.3 Rounding Policy
All gross quotations are rounded down to the nearest HK$10 to simplify payment processing.

**Example:**
- Pre-round gross: HK$53,247
- Rounded (floored to HK$10): HK$53,240


### 1.4 Fee Update Schedule
Platform fees and adjustment matrices may be updated at any time at the sole discretion of Fitout Hub. Changes become effective 14 days after notification to all stakeholders. Historical quotes retain their pricing under the rules in force at time of submission.


### 1.5 No Refund of Platform Fee
Once a quote is accepted and a contract is formed, the platform fee is non-refundable. In cases of disputed scope or cancellation, platform fees are determined per the contract terms and applicable refund policies.


## 2. Financial Transparency (New Subsection under Payments)

### 2.1 Quote vs. Invoice
- **Quote Gross Price**: Includes platform fee, presented to client
- **Professional Payout**: Based on their submitted base amount (or as negotiated)
- **Platform Revenue**: Retains the calculated platform fee

The platform fee is separate from any service fees, transaction fees, or other charges that may apply to escrow, payment processing, or disputes.


## 3. Amendment to "Pricing & Fees" Section (If Exists)

Add the following clarification:

*Professionals agree that quotations submitted through the Platform may be subject to platform fees as outlined in Section 1 (Platform Fee Disclosure). The presence of a platform fee does not obligate professionals to accept lower awards or affect their ability to negotiate directly with the client outside of the Platform.*


## 4. Professional Disclosures (New Section)

### 4.1 Transparency to Professionals
- Professionals will be shown their base quote and the calculated gross price before final submission
- The fee breakdown (base band, adjustments, effective percent, fee amount) will be displayed in the submission confirmation
- Professionals may review but cannot modify the fee calculation; it is applied uniformly

### 4.2 Historical Data
- Professionals can view their awarded project count and current tier in their dashboard
- Clients can optionally view their loyalty tier in their profile settings


## 5. Client Disclosures (New Section)

### 5.1 Transparency to Clients
- Clients see only the gross quoted price (all fees included)
- Fee breakdowns are available in admin/internal views only (not shown to clients by design)
- Clients are not billed separately for platform fees; they are included in the quoted work price


## 6. Dispute & Exception Handling

Platform fees are subject to the general dispute resolution process outlined in the main T&Cs. If a party disputes the fee calculation (e.g., professional tier or client loyalty count is incorrect), they may raise a support case within 30 days of quote submission, and the platform will review and reconcile as appropriate.


## 7. Effective Date & Transition

- **Effective Date**: [INSERT DATE]
- **Existing Quotes**: Quotes submitted before the effective date are NOT subject to platform fees; they retain their original pricing
- **New Quotes**: All quotes submitted on or after the effective date are subject to platform fees per the above terms
- **Revised Quotes**: If a professional revises a quote after the effective date, the new fee structure applies
